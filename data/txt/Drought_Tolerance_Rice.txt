Title: Identification of SNP Markers Associated with Drought Tolerance in Rice

Authors: Plant Genomics Research Group

Publication Date: March 2023

Summary

This study identified twelve significant SNP markers associated with drought tolerance in rice using genome-wide association analysis. The research evaluated 450 rice accessions under controlled drought stress. Four markers located on chromosome 4 consistently showed strong association with grain yield under water-limited conditions.

Key Findings

Identified 12 significant SNP markers.
Four markers showed consistent performance across multiple environments.
Grain yield improved by approximately 18% using marker-assisted selection.
Candidate genes related to root architecture were discovered.
